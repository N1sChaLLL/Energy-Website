Leading Gas Distribution Companies in UAE

As one of the foremost gas distribution companies in the UAE, Energy Next delivers end-to-end distribution expertise across three core market segments:

Industrial gas distribution

Our turnkey industrial gas distribution services power factories, warehouses, and process facilities...

We engineer high-capacity manifolds and large-bore piping to handle heavy-duty flow rates, ensuring your production lines never pause. From bulk LPG storage tanks to custom vaporisation units, we provide robust solutions that meet stringent international standards.

Commercial gas distribution

For hotels, restaurants, hospitals, and retail centers, our commercial gas distribution systems prioritize flexibility and safety. Utilizing centralized meter rooms and fault-tolerant manifold assemblies, we maintain consistent supply even during peak demand. Our certified technicians execute seamless changeovers and pressure-balancing controls to protect both end-users and equipment.

Residential gas distribution

Energy Next brings safe, cost-effective residential gas distribution to high-rise apartments, villas, and gated communities. By integrating compact cylinder rack systems and apartment-level regulators, we provide uninterrupted kitchen and heating gas supply. Our “no-dig” trenchless installations and low-profile piping ensure minimal disruption to landscaping and common areas.

Across all segments, we leverage advanced gas pipeline installation techniques and proactive gas network management to optimize flow efficiency, minimize leak risk, and extend system lifespan.
