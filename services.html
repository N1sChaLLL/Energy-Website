Services

Front-End Engineering Design (FEED)

Our Front-End Engineering Design (FEED) service ensures a future-ready gas system tailored to your needs. By optimizing system design to meet current and future demands while adhering to local regulations, FEED minimizes changes during the EPC phase and reduces costs. It also provides a strong foundation for project bidding and serves as a benchmark for efficient, on-time execution.

Consultation

We transform conceptual gas system designs into final plans, ensuring compliance with regulations, international codes, and quality standards. Our Value Engineering approach optimizes sustainability while keeping projects on budget, with close monitoring at every phase for seamless execution.

Central Gas System Installation

We specialize in installing gas systems for Natural Gas (NG), Substitute Natural Gas (SNG), and Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) across diverse projects, including residential towers, hotels, malls, schools, restaurants, villas, industrial facilities, labor camps, and hospitals. Our services also include cylinder manifold systems and advanced gas leak detection systems, ensuring safety and reliability in every project.


Energy Next is one of the U.A.E’s leading and best gas company, specialising in distribution systems for Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG), Synthetic Natural Gas (SNG) and Natural Gas (NG). Headquartered in Dubai since 2024.

After Sales

24x7 Emergency Call Response Centre

We operate a 24/7 Emergency Call Response Center to ensure immediate assistance during gas-related emergencies. Our dedicated team prioritizes your safety and system functionality, swiftly coordinating corrective actions with the appropriate personnel for prompt resolution.

Gas System Training

We provide comprehensive training for customers and facility management teams to ensure safe gas system operations during emergencies. Our briefings cover essential dos and don’ts for handling gas interruptions or leaks, enhancing awareness and promoting safe usage of the systems.

Cost Saving Strategies

Cost plays a key role in customer satisfaction, and we focus on delivering efficient, reliable gas systems that minimize long-term expenses. By assessing feasibility and converting existing systems to optimized solutions, we help reduce future costs while ensuring compliance with safety regulations. Our goal is to propose cost-effective systems that meet financial needs without compromising quality or safety.

Project Management

We specialize in delivering central gas systems for large-scale developments, managing every phase to ensure projects are completed on time, within budget, and in compliance with quality and regulatory standards. Our comprehensive approach ensures a seamless and stress-free experience, allowing you to focus on your project's success.
